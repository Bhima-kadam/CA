Corporate Governance and Secretarial Excellence

Corporate Secretarial Services

Managing a company involves strict adherence to the Companies Act, 2013. We act as your extended corporate secretarial team, ensuring that all statutory records and filings are updated correctly with the Registrar of Companies (ROC).

Our Corporate Services:

  • Company Incorporation: End-to-end setup of Pvt Ltd, OPC, and LLP entities.
  • Annual ROC Filings: Preparation and filing of Form AOC-4 and MGT-7.
  • Event-Based Filings: Managing changes in Directors, Share Capital, or Registered Office.
  • Maintenance of Registers: Statutory registers, minute books, and share certificates.
  • KYC Compliance: Annual DIR-3 KYC and active company tagging (INC-22A).
