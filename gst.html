Goods & Services Tax (GST)

Seamless Compliance and Input Tax Credit Optimization

End-to-End GST Solutions

Since the implementation of GST, the tax landscape has become tech-driven and highly regulated. We help businesses stay ahead of the curve by managing their entire GST lifecycle, from registration to annual audits.

Our GST Offerings:

  • GST Registration: New registrations and amendments for all business types.
  • Periodic Returns: Filing of GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and GSTR-4 (Composition).
  • ITC Reconciliation: Matching GSTR-2B/2A with purchase registers to ensure no credit loss.
  • Annual Returns: Preparation and filing of GSTR-9 and GSTR-9C.
  • GST Refunds: Assistance in claiming refunds for exporters and inverted duty structures.
